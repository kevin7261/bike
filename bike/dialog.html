DATA SOURCE
The basic infomation and rental data of YouBike was from Public bike sharing system in Taipei (臺北市公共自行車租賃系統) and Rental records of YouBike in Taipei (臺北市自行車租借紀錄). The weather data was from CWB Observation Data Inquire System (觀測資料查詢系統). We mainly analyze the data in 2019.
